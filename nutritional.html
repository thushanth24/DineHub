Nutritional information

Understanding Nutritional Information

we believe in providing our valued guests with the information they need to make informed dining choices. Here's a brief guide to help you understand the nutritional information provided for our menu items


Calories: This measures the energy content of the food. It indicates how much energy you can expect to get from consuming a serving of the dish.


Macronutrients:
Protein: Helps build and repair tissues.
Carbohydrates: Main source of energy for the body.
Fat: Essential for various bodily functions.


Micronutrients:
Vitamins: Essential for various bodily functions such as immune support and bone health.
Minerals: Important for metabolism, bone health, and other bodily functions.


Allergens: We provide information about common allergens present in our dishes, such as gluten, dairy, nuts, and shellfish. This helps guests with food allergies make safe choices.


Daily Values (%DV): The percentage of the Daily Value (%DV) indicates how much a serving of the food contributes to your daily recommended intake of various nutrients, based on a 2,000-calorie diet


Portion Sizes: Pay attention to the portion sizes listed, as they can affect the nutritional content of the dish.


Please note that the nutritional information provided is based on standard recipes and serving sizes. Actual values may vary depending on factors such as customization and preparation methods. If you have specific dietary concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to ask our staff. We're here to help you enjoy a delicious meal that fits your nutritional needs and preferences.
